## THE MATH

### Bonus Bet Conversion (How Tier 1 Works)

Bonus bets are "stake not returned" — you only keep the profit, not the bet amount.

**Example: Converting a $200 DraftKings bonus bet**

1. You have a $200 bonus bet on DraftKings
2. OddsJam finds: Underdog at +400 on DraftKings, Favorite at -420 on bet365
3. Place $200 BONUS BET on Underdog at +400 (DraftKings)
4. Place $615 CASH HEDGE on Favorite at -420 (bet365)

**If Underdog wins:**
- DraftKings pays: $800 profit (stake not returned)
- bet365 loses: -$615
- **Net profit: +$185**

**If Favorite wins:**
- DraftKings loses: $0 (it was a bonus bet, you lose nothing)
- bet365 pays: $146 profit
- **Net profit: +$146**

**Guaranteed profit: $146-$185 regardless of outcome.**
**Conversion rate: 73-93% of the $200 bonus = excellent.**

OddsJam does this math for you automatically and tells you exactly where to place each side.

**Either way you profit $214-$634.** The hedge costs you some upside but eliminates downside.

### Kelly Criterion (How Tier 3 Works)

For real-money +EV bets, never risk more than 5% of bankroll on one bet.

```
Kelly fraction = (edge / odds) * 0.25    (quarter Kelly for safety)
Max bet = min(Kelly fraction * bankroll, bankroll * 0.05)

Example: Model says 60% chance, odds are +150
Edge = 0.60 - 0.40 = 0.20 (20% edge)
Quarter Kelly = (0.20 / 1.50) * 0.25 = 3.3%
Bet = $2,000 * 0.033 = $66
Capped at 5% = $100 max
Bet: $66
```

## GLOSSARY

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Bonus bet** | Free bet from sportsbook. Stake not returned — you only keep profit |
| **Qualifying bet** | Small real-money bet placed to unlock a bonus offer |
| **Hedge** | Bet on the opposite outcome at a different book to guarantee profit |
| **Conversion rate** | % of bonus bet face value you extract as cash (typically 70-80%) |
| **Edge** | Difference between model probability and market implied probability |
| **Kelly criterion** | Formula for optimal bet sizing based on edge and odds |
| **Implied probability** | What the odds suggest the win chance is (e.g., -110 = 52.4%) |
| **+EV** | Positive expected value — a bet where you have a mathematical edge |
| **Vig/juice** | Sportsbook's commission built into the odds (typically 4-5%) |
| **First bet insurance** | Sportsbook refunds your first bet as bonus bets if it loses |
| **Profit boost** | Token that increases your payout (e.g., 100% boost doubles profit) |
| **Matched betting** | Hedging strategy that guarantees profit from promotional offers |
| **Arb/arbitrage** | Betting both sides across books where odds are mispriced for guaranteed profit |
| **Line movement** | When odds change due to sharp money or new information |
